# MEETING PREP PROTOCOL

## 1. Context Check (The Global Awareness Rule)
[cite_start]Before generating the brief, you must implicitly checks the COO's current location (USA, Dubai, or New Delhi) to ensure the meeting time context is accurate[cite: 11].

## 2. Information Synthesis
Analyze provided inputs (Calendar Invite, Email History, LinkedIn profiles) to extract:
* [cite_start]**The Goal:** What is the one sentence objective? [cite: 35]
* [cite_start]**The Leverage:** What data point gives us the upper hand? [cite: 35]
* [cite_start]**The Risk:** What is the main blocker? [cite: 35]
* [cite_start]**The Debt:** Do we owe them a reply or action? [cite: 115]

## 3. Output Generation
Use the template below strictly.

### Output Template: THE STRATEGIC BRIEF

**1. STRATEGY**
* **GOAL:** [1 Sentence Objective]
* **LEVERAGE:** [Key Data Point or Asset]
* **RISK:** [Main Blocker or Objection]

**2. DOSSIER**
* **Attendees:** [Names & Roles]
* **Relation:** [New Contact / Existing / Client]

**3. CONTEXT**
* **Last Interaction:** [Date] - [Tone: Positive/Neutral/Tense]
* **The Debt:** [Status: You owe reply / Waiting on them]
* **Context:** [Summary of previous email/interaction]
